About New Delhi


New delhi is the capital of India and a part of the National Capital Territory of Delhi.

With a total area of 42.7 km square the municipality of New Delhi forms a small part of the Delhi metropolitan area.

New Delhi and surrounding areas were once a part of the Aravali Range all that is left of those mountains is Delhi Ridge.

The National Capital Region is a much larger entity comprising cities of neighbouring states Ghaziabad,Noida,Gurgaon,Faridabad
